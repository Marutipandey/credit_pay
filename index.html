AEPS

Aadhaar enabled Payment Systems (AePS) is an easy technique for people to access their bank account by simply authenticating their Aadhaar number using their biometrics. No complicated documentation and number crunching is needed to execute transactions using AePS. Customers can avail of various banking services like Money Transfer, Balance Enquiry and Cash Withdrawal without having to use any card or cheque.

BBPS

its Adhikaris to accept payments for all utility bills such as water, electricity, Gas, and Broadband through the Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS). The BBPS service also facilitates other payments for services like FasTag, Municipal Corporation Tax, LIC Premiums and much more.

DMT

Money transfer services are something that we all use in our everyday lives. In places where banking services are a scarce resource, money transfer can become a hassle. To eliminate this problem, trusst payoffers Domestic Money Transfer API that can help its users transfer money Pan India without any hassle. With our 99% success rate, all the transfers made with trusst pay’s Domestic Money Transfer API are secure and foolproof.

Mobile and DTH Recharge

The ongoing sensation of the internet has increased the usage of mobile phones exponentially in India. With the wide spread of Digital Mobile recharge options, it has become difficult for people residing in remote areas of India, with very little and sometimes no connectivity to access easy Mobile Recharge facilities. To counter this problem, trusst payhas developed Mobile Recharge API that can help small businesses located in these areas to offer easy and accessible mobile recharge to their customers.

Aadhaar Pay

Aadhaar Pay, Adhikaris can accept cashless payments from their customers, simply with an Aadhaar number and a fingerprint scanner. This system of fast, secure, and seamless payment management service gives both the customers and our Adhikaris the freedom from day-to-day cash management.

Pan Card Services

PAN Cards have become more prominent in the recent past, as the government has mandated using PAN Card for most official purposes. With trusst pay’s PAN Card API, businesses can now assist their clients to acquire their PAN Card without having to go through the hassle of long queues and confusing documentation.
